QuadFrost
by Napoleon

Background: Hidden away in a foreign land,
there are enemies and airstrikes. Fight your
way through a frozen tundra military base.
Survive the frost throughout each quad.
A "DoubleFrost" nightmare at high resolution.
This is QuadFrost.
